✨ The Inspiring Journey

The Heart Behind Mayookha Boutique, Saree Rolling and Dry Cleaners

Mayookha Boutique, Saree Rolling and Dry Cleaners is built upon a foundation of remarkable courage, resilience, and pure self-belief.

Our founder, Geethamma Devarenti, is the soul of everything we do. Having lost her husband at the tender age of 20, she stood tall against life's heaviest storms, raising her daughter as a single mother with unwavering determination.

Despite facing immense hardships, she refused to let her dreams fade. Guided by a deep passion for traditional saree care and fashion, she worked consistently through the years, turning challenges into stepping stones to establish a trusted business that stands for quality and trust.

Geethamma's inspiring journey of perseverance remains the motivation and heartbeat behind our dedication to preserving your most cherished memories.

A Legacy of Love

The Story Behind Our Name

Our business, Mayookha Boutique, Saree Rolling and Dry Cleaners, is named after Mayookha Bhumireddy, the cherished granddaughter of our founder, Geethamma Devarenti.

Just like little Mayookha brings brightness and joy to our lives, we strive to bring the same beauty and elegance to your wardrobe. Naming our boutique after her represents our promise of pure love, dedication, and care in every stitch and fold.
